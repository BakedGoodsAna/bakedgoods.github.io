About us

We are based in Cluj Napoca, but we deliver everywhere in Romania.


All our products are made with natural ingredients from local small business.

Read more

We launch a special collection of desserts for every holiday.


Ingredients

We try to support other local small business from Cluj-Napoca. To do this we purchase most of our ingredients from local markets such as Piața Ira, Piața Flora and Piața Abator. 

We also collaborate with: Lactate Lechinta, Siamond, Hari Berry, Stupina ApiVio and Ferma Steluța.

This is the reason our fruit desserts differ from season to season. We think fresh fruits and ingredients add an amazing touch to our products, which makes them special!
